Some Informations About Nayantara Plant

Scientific Name Catharanthus Roseus

 or

Vincea Rosea
Common Name Sada Bahar
Periwinkle
Nayantara
Family Name Apocyanace
Parts Used Whole Plant
Medicinal Use
  • Regulates Diabetes
  • Remedies for Respiratory Anomalies
  • Manages High Blood Pressure or Hypertension
  • Improves Cognitive Functioning
  • Augments Skin Health
  • Regulates Menstrual Flow
  • Facilitates Wound Healing
  • Very useful in Leukemia & Hodgkin's Lymphoma
Side Effects When taken by mouth directly, it can cause side effects due to excessive taking like nausea, vomiting, hair loss, hearing loss, dizziness, low blood pressure, nerve problems, seizures, low blood sugar and even death. So it is highly recommended to an Ayurvedic Doctor or Practitioner before consuming it.
