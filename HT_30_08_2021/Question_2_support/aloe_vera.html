Some Informations About Aloe-Vera Plant

Scientific Name Aloe Barbadensis Miller
Common Name Grithokumari
Burn Plant
Medicinal Aloe
Barbados Aloe
Family Name Liliaceae
Parts Used Leaves
Medicinal Use
  • Best known for treating skin injuries
  • It has antioxidant & antibacterial properties
  • Accelerates wound healing
  • Reduces dental plaque
  • Used to treat Canker sores
  • Reduces constipation
  • Improves skin & prevent wrinkles
  • Lowers blood sugar levels
Side Effects
  • Excessively low blood sugar in long-term use.
  • Burning & Itching of the skin (infrequent, depends on skin types)
  • Stomach pain and Cramps (due to high does)
  • Diarrhea, Kidney Problems, Blood in Urine, Low Potassium, Muscle Weakness, Weight Loss and Heart Disturbances (long-term use in high does)
  • Liver Problems (Rare)
