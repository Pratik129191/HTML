Medicinal Use
  • Stone Problems:  Leaves of this plant is very good for removing kidney stone and multiple small gall bladder stone, by Chewing leaves (2-3) or extract leaves juice and drinking twice a day.
  • Astringent: Causing the contraction of the skin cells and other body tissues and helps to stop bleeding.
  • Can be used as an analgesic.
  • Decrease platelet aggregation and inhibit thrombus formation.
  • Helps in relieving or preventing dysentery.
  • Reduces inflammation by acting on body mechanisms.
  • Capable of preventing infection by inhibiting the growth of infectious agents.
  • Tending to arrest or prevent cancer due to Cytotoxic nature
  • Promotes excretion of urine/agent that increases the amount of urine excreted.
  • Can be used as Emollient for Soothing and softening effect on the skin or an irritated internal surface.
  • Modifies the immune response or the functioning of the immune system.
  • capable of causing bleeding to stop when it is applied to a wound.
Side Effects Side effects tend to be mild and may include stomach upset and diarrhea due to long-term use. This plant should be avoided in children, pregnant women, and nursing mothers. It should also be avoided in people with Wilson's disease as it may further reduce uric acid levels and increase the risk of liver damage.
